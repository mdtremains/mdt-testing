A symbol is a sign or a simple, elemental visual that stands for or represents 
another things beyond its immediate meaning. Well designed symbols convey information, 
express meaning and are quickly recognized. A distinct sign also is appropriate for the 
idea or thing that it represents, can be reproduced in color and black & white, and can be 
reproduced at various sizes. Goal: to explore conceptual development skills and to 
understand the functional purpose of a symbol as representative of communication.

Parameters Include: the symbol must be reproduced with reductive language 
(black & white, basic shapes, etc.), display three various sizes of symbol on separate sheet of paper.
